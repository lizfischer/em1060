Mark Faulkner

Mark Faulkner will be Lecturer in Old English at University College Cork until 1 September 2012, when he becomes Lecturer in Medieval Literature at the University of Sheffield. Mark's publications on twelfth-century literature include 'Archaism, Belatedness and Modernisation: "Old" English in the Twelfth Century' (Review of English Studies), 'Rewriting English Literary History, 1042-1215' (Literature Compass) and 'Gerald of Wales and Standard Old English' (Notes and Queries). He is currently working on a new literary history entitled Ignota lingua: English literatures in the long twelfth century.
