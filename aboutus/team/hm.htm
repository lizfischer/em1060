Hollie Morgan

Hollie is currently studying for an interdisciplinary D.Phil at the Centre for Medieval Studies, York, under the supervision of Dr Nicola McDonald and Dr Jeremy Goldberg. Using a range of documentary, literary, archaeological and material sources, her thesis explores the cultural meanings of beds and chambers in late medieval England. Hollie completed her MA in English Literary Research in 2010, having conducted a detailed study of London, College of Arms, Arundel 58 under the supervision of Dr Orietta Da Rold. She is interested in pursuing and promoting interdisciplinary research, particularly in regards to material culture.
