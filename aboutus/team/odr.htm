Dr Orietta Da Rold

Orietta is University Lecturer in Literature and the Material Text: 1100 to 1500 at the University of Cambridge and Fellow at St John's college, Cambridge. She researches medieval literature c. 1100-1500, focusing in particular on the codicology and palaeography of medieval manuscripts, and their manifestation in the electronic age. She has published on twelfth-century English manuscripts, the use of medieval paper in Britain and the textual culture of Chaucer's manuscripts.
