Names of the winds in English and Latin

Date: s. xii/xiii

Summary:

Manuscript contains Old English and Latin names of winds which have been included on fol. 150v, 'the last leaf of a manuscript containing works of Ivo of Chartres, s. xii' (Ker 1957, p. 331). The manuscript is of unknown origins.

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fol. 150v
    Title (B.24.5): Names of the Winds

    Incipit: Subsolanus uel ab oriente. estan

    Explicit: Thaurus. estan norðan

    Text Language: Latin with English names

    Bibliography:

    Ker 1957, item 261
