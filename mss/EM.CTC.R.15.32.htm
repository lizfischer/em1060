Note requesting a copy of verse

Date: s. xiex

Summary:

Note (Item 13) in the margin of a manuscript of Latin computistical texts. The note is transcribed by Ker thus: '7 ic bidde ðe þæt þu me writ þas VII uers 7 send me' (1957, p. 135). Ker notes that it refers to the Ratio certa de terminis tenenda and the De epacta et de concurrenti ratio in the main text on the same page. Main text written in Winchester, s. xiin; marginal hand from s. xiex is not localised.

Names of the months (Items 1-12) in a calender were added after 1025.
