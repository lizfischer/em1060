Ælfric's Grammar

Date: s. ximed

Summary:

Ælfric's Grammar and Glossary. Incomplete, probably due to two missing quires. Written by two hands, ximed, Ker notes that the manuscript is 'possibly Kentish' (1957, p. 303).
