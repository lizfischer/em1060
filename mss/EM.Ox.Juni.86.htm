Homilies

Date: s. ximed

Summary:

Three homilies, fragments, owing to the loss of leaves after and before quires. The manuscript is part of Junius 85. Origin unknown.

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fols 36-40
    Title (B.3.5.5): Anonymous Homilies [for unspecified occasions, listed under the opening words]: Geherað nu men þa leofestan hu us godes bec

    Incipit: (fol. 36) continuation of Junius 85

    Explicit: (fol. 40r) wuniað ðon[ne] mid criste | ðam ðe nu lyfað. 7 ricsað. mid god | fæder. ðam sie wuldor. 7 lof. a in eal|ra. worulda. woruld. a buten ænde

    Bibliography:

    Fadda 1977, pp. 6-31

    Ker 1957, pp. 409-11

    Wilcox 2008, pp. 113-28
