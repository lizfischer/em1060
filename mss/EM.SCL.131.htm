Scribble

Date: s. xi/xii

Summary:

Main text is Ephraem Syrus. The main text scribe has written in a margin a note which has no relationship to the main text. Ker identifies the scribe as ‘a Salisbury scribe whose work is known in a number of manuscripts’ (1957, p. 577) .

Manuscript Items:

    Title (B.27.3.45.EM): Scribbles
