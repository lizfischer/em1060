Gloss to Prayers

Date: s. ximed

Summary:

Interlinear gloss to 40 Latin prayers and forms of confession which follow a Roman psalter. Ker 1957 notes that the gloss is written by two hands almost contemporarty to the main hand, s. ximed (p. 171). Calendar and litany are localizable to Christ Church, Canterbury.
