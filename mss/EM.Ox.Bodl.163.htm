Cædmon's Hymn, and Latin-Old English glossary

Date: s. ximed

Summary:

Cædmon's Hymn appears as an addition in the margin of a copy of Bede's Historia Ecclesiastia, which contains also four glosses in Old English. A Latin-Old English glossary on fols 250-51. Ker (1957, p. 358) observes that the 'manuscript appears to have been at Peterborough by s. xiiin'.
