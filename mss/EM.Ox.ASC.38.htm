Ælfric's Grammar

Date: s. ximed

Summary:

Ælfric's Grammar on fols 1-12 and part of Royal 12. G. xii, fols 2-9. The scribe who copied the text on fols 1-12 of the current manuscript also copied fols 7 and 8 of Royal 12. G. xii (Ker 1957, p. 334). Origin unknown.

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fols 1-12
    Title (B.1.9.1): Ælfric's Grammar

    Incipit: (fol. 9) ab

    Explicit: (fol. 12) ðam

    Text Language: English

    Note: According to Ker, the correct order of leaves should be fols 9, 10, 7, 8, 1-6, 11, 12, hence the incipit on fol. 9 (1957, p. 333).

    Bibliography:

    Ker 1957, item 265
