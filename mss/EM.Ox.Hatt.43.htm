Cædmon’s Hymn

Date: s. xi2

Summary:

Main text is an s. xiin copy of the Latin version of Bede’s Historia ecclesiastica, written in English Caroline minuscule. Cædmon’s Hymn is added on the bottom margin of fol. 129. Place of production not known.

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fol. 129
    Title (A.32.2): Cædmon's Hymn: West Saxon Version

    Incipit: (fol. 129) Nu we sculon herian heofonrices weard

    Explicit: (fol. 129) Æfter tida firum on foldum frea ælmyhtig

    Text Language: English

    Bibliography:

    Ker 1957, item 326

Administration Information:

Manuscript described by Mary Swan and Owen Roberson with the assistance of Hollie Morgan, with reference to published scholarship (2012).
