Cædmon’s Hymn and Bede’s Death Song

Date: s. xiimed

Summary:

Cædmon’s Hymn and Bede’s Death Song, which was added, in a hand roughly contemporary with that of the main text, in Old English to ruled lines in the bottom margins of fols 83r and 112v of a s. xiimed copy of the Latin text of the Historia ecclesiastica. Place of production not known.
