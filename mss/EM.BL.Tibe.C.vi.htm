Gloss; Homily

Date: s. ximed

Summary:

Continuous Old English gloss to the Gallican Version of the Psalter. The preliminary quires also contain two entries in English from s. ximed: a homily, De septiformi spitritu, and a heading in red rustic capitals.

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fols 29v-30r
    Title (B.1.6.3): Ælfric, Tracts: De Septiformi Spiritu [Be þam halgan gaste]

    Incipit: (fol. 29v) Her is þæt ylce on ænglisc. Isaias se ƿitega aƿrat on his ƿitegunge be þam halgan gaste

    Explicit: (fols 29v-30r) hƿæðer him goddes gast on ƿunige || oððe þæs gramlican deofles

    Text Language: English

  2. Item: fol. 30r
    Title (B.12.4.10.3): Rubrics and Directions for the use of Forms of Service

    Addition: (fol. 30r) Ðis gebed Baeda sang æt ðam saltere

    Text Language: English

  3. Item: fols 31r-129v
    Title (C.7.6): Gloss to Psalms [Gallican version]

    Incipit: (fol. 31r) (incipit illegible)

    Explicit: (fol. 129v) þa þe andrædað hihte on drihten gefylsta heora ˥ gefyld is

    Text Language: English
