Glosses

Date: s. ximed

Summary:

Glosses to Aldhelm's De laude virginitatis (fols 1r-95) and a letter to Ehrfrid (fols 95v-100) with an Abingdon provenance, s. xex. About thirty glosses are contemporary with the texts; glosses on fols 8-15 are probably s. xiin; and the glosses to more than 5,4000 words in De laude virginitatis in fols 8-95 are s. ximed (Ker 1957, p. 382).
