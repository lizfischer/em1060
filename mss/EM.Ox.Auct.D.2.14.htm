Booklist and list of names

Date: s. xi2

Summary:

A list of books, mostly service-books, followed by a list of names, all in English, added on a leaf bound in with a s. vii gospel-book. Book-list is in two hands and list of names in a third. Ker (1957, p. 350) suggests that the leaf might come from ‘the end of a service-book of Bury St. Edmunds’.
