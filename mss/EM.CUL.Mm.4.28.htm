Godric’s prayer

Date: s. xii/xiii

Summary:

Annotation in Latin and Old English on a blank space on the recto of final leaf of an s. xii2 manuscript of Latin Vitas patrum, after a note in Latin about Godric’s hymn which Ker judges to be ‘in a different, but contemporary hand’. Ker also notes an inscription on fol. 1, possibly of s. xiiex, from Biddlesden Priory, Bucks (1957, p. 40).

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fol. 149

      Title (A.46): Godric's Prayer

      Addition: (fol. 149) Sancte marie uirgine moder iesu cristes nazarene onfo scild help þin godric onfang bring hehtlic þið þe i godes Riche
