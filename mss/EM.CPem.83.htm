List of Payments

Date: s. xi/xii

Summary:

A list of payments relating to an Anglo-Scandinavian burial and funeral feasts, at the top of a flyleaf of a copy of Bede's Latin commentary of St. Luke. Bury St Edmunds by the time of the s. xiv ex-libris inscription.

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fol. 1r
    Title (B.16.5.3): Bury: Payments at a Burial

    Addition: (fol. 1r)

    • 7 twælf oræn. Under prestæs [...] (Paragraph 1 of list of payments)
    • 7 fyf oræ ate fyrræ ærflæ [...] (Paragraph 2 of list of payments)
    • 7 half twælf ere ate hoþær hærflæ (Paragraph 3 of list of payments)

    Text Language: English

    Bibliography:

    Ker 1957, pp. 124-25
