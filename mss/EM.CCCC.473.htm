Scribble

Date: s. xi2

Summary:

Note in English and Latin in blank space on fol. 197, at the end of the 'Winchester Troper' (the manuscript is from s. xi1). From St. Swithun's cathedral priory, Winchester.

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fol. 197r

      Title (B.27.3.6): Scribbles

      Addition: (fol. 197r) Unum legio est. sex milia et sexcenti ac sexaginta sex uiri. an legio iss. six þusend ˥ six hundred ˥ six and sixtig

      Bibliography:

        Ker 1957, item 72

        Frere 1894, p. 98
