Glosses to Aldhelm's prose treatise

Date: s. ximed

Summary:

Numerous glosses are added to Aldhelm's De laude virginitatis, written in s. x/xi. Some by contemporary or near contemporary hands, some in s. xiin, and some in ximed.

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fols 1-120

      Title (C.31.7): Gloss to Aldhelm, De laude virginitatis

      Text Language: English and Latin

      Date: Eighty-two out of ca. 700 Old English interlinear and marginal glosses are from s. ximed.

      Hand: In 'rougher script' than earlier hands (Ker 1957).

      Bibliography:

        Pulsiano 1996

        Ker 1957, p. 321, i(c)

Administration Information:

    Manuscript described by Takako Kato with the assistance of Sanne van der Schee, with reference to published scholarship (2010; 2012).
