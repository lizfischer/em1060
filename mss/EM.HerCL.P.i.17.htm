Glosses

Date: s. xii/xiii

Summary:

57 English glosses to Aldhelm’s prose De laude virginitatis, of the same date as the main text, and added after Latin glosses to it. Flyleaf inscription localises manuscript to Cirencester (Ker 1957, p. 157).

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fols 2v-94v
    Title (C.31.4): Gloss to Aldhelm, De laude virginitatis

    Addition: (fol. 2v) ad pontificale, episcopale, biscoplicum / conciliabulum colloquium synoðe

    Addition: (fol. 94v) indicium, dom

    Note: Fifty-seven glosses to Aldhelm, De laude virginitatis, in the prose version

    Bibliography:

    Napier 1900, no.3, pp. 149-50

Administration Information:

Manuscript described by Mary Swan and Owen Roberson with the assistance of Johanna Green, with reference to published scholarship.
