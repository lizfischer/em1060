Byrhtferth of Ramsey's Handbook

Date: s. ximed

Summary:

Computus handbook written in Latin and Old English, and two homiletic pieces. One hand, origin unknown.

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: pp. 1-247
    Title (B.20.20.1): Byrhtferth's Manual

    Incipit: in capite (The contents of pp. 1-54 are disarranged.)

    Text Language: English

    Note: Inscription relating to a diagram: 'byrhtferð monachus ramesiensis' on the seventh leaf of the first quire (a fragment).

    Bibliography:

    Ker 1957, item 288

  2. Item: pp. 247-51
    Title (B.3.4.38): Anonymous Homilies [for unspecified occasions, published]: Napier 1883 [1967], no. 47: 'Larspell and scriftboc'

    Incipit (Latin): (p. 247) Iohannes ait. post mille annos.

    Incipit: (p. 247) Se halga iohannes cwæð æfter þam þusende byð se deofol unbunden

    Text Language: English

    Bibliography:

    Ker 1957, item 288
