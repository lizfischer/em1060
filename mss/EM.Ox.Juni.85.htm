Homilies

Date: s. ximed

Summary:

Five homilies, fragments, owing to the loss of leaves after and before quires. The manuscript is part of Junius 86. Origin unknown.

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fol. 2r
    Title (B.3.2.40): Anonymous Homilies, Homilies for Specified Occasions, Temporale: Tuesday in Rogationtide

    Incipit: (fol. 2r) song 7 godes lof [...] | stan cynincges

    Explicit: (fol. 2r) soðlice buton | ænde; AMEN

    Note: Fragment

    Bibliography:

    Ker 1957, pp. 409-11

    Wilcox 2008, pp. 113-28.
