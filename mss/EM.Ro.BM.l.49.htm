Note

Date: s. xii

Summary:

A note which was added in Old English by the main hand in a margin of a copy of the Latin text of the Historia ecclesiastica. Place of production not known.

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fol. 95v

      Title (B.24.28.EM): Miscellaneous Additional Notes

      Addition: (fol. 95v) romeburg geitmbred were from frimðe weron agane feower ðusend wintra 7 feor hund. 7 twa 7 hund eahtatig. 7 æfter þem þe rome getimbroð wes wes ures drihtnes acenes ymb seofon hund wintre 7 tiene

      Note: Possible Orosius? [OR]

      Bibliography:

        Ker 1957, p. 448
