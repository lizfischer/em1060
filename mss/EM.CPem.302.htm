Boundaries of the see of Hereford

Date: s. xi2

Summary:

16 lines of text in English on the recto of a formerly blank leaf (fol. 8) of a Latin evangeliary written in England probably in s. ximed (Ker 1957, p. 125). Manuscript at Hereford Cathedral in s. xi.

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fol. 8r

      Title (B.16.11.1): Hereford: Boundaries of See

      Title (manuscript): Hanc discretionem fecit æthestanus episcopus

      Incipit: (fol. 8r) Ðus ligð þæt bisceoprice into hereforda

      Text Language: English

      Note: Old English text is sixteen lines.

      Bibliography:

        Ker 1957, pp. 125-26

Administration Information:

    Manuscript described by Mary Swan and Owen Roberson, with the assistance of Sanne van der Schee; ed. by Takako Kato (2010; 2013).
