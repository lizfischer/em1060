Lives of Saints

Date: s. ximed

Summary:

A composite manuscript which contains music and hymns in Latin and Saints' Lives in English. The Lives of Saints is contained in the second part of the manuscript on fols 93-130v. Ker (1957) notes one hand, s. ximed. Origin unknown, belonged to Thomas Allen.
