Glosses in OE and Latin to Aldhelm, prose De laude virginitatis

Date: s. xii2

Summary:

Six OE glosses of the same date as the main text and its Latin glosses. Ker transcribes the inscription on fol. 1v of s. xii/xiii 'Liber sancta marie noui monasterii', and notes ‘i.e. the Cistercian abbey of Newminster, near Morpeth in Northumberland’ (1957, p. 309).
