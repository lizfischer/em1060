Cædmon’s Hymn

Date: s. xiimed

Summary:

Cædmon’s Hymn which is added in Old English by the main hand in a margin of a copy of the Latin text of the Historia ecclesiastica. Place of production not known. The hand has some similar characteristics to that of a scribe in Bodley 343, fols 1-170.
