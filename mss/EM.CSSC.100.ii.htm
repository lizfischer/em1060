Liturgical directions

Date: s. ximed

Summary:

Directorial annotations, s. ximed, which are written in the margins of Ordo de sacris ordinibus benedicendis (s. xi1). 'liber sancti cuthberti' is written on the first page by a s. xii/xiii hand in Durham (Ker 1957, p. 128).
