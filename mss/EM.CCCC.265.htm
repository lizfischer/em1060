Directions for a confessor; Law; Glosses

Date: s. xi2

Summary:

A collection of texts relating to canon law. The Old English items comprise:

  • pp. 72-83 Directions for a confessor, as in Brussels, Bibliothèque Royale, 8558-63, beginning 'Ðæt sceal geþencan se þe bið manna sawla læce';
  • pp. 222-27 The set of laws known as IV Eadgar, which according to Ker 1957 agrees closely with that of Nero E. i;
  • pp. 30-112 Glosses in several hands to the Ecgberht Poenitentiale and the Penitentiale Theodori (Ker 1957, pp. 92-94).
From the script Ker 1957 suggests that the manuscript was copied in Worcester. A s. xi2 hand writes 'domno presule uulstano presente'. Ker 1957 notes: ' the reference must be to Bishop Wulfstan II (1062-95)'.
