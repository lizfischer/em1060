Cædmon’s Hymn

Date: s. xii1

Summary:

Contemporary addition in English to the margin of the Latin text of Bede’s Historia ecclesiastica. Place of production not known.

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fol. 116v
    Title (A.32.2): Cædmon's Hymn: West Saxon Version

    Addition: (fol. 116v) Nu ƿe sceolan herian hel[o]fon rices ƿeardmetudesmihteondhis mod[g]e þanc ƿeorc ƿulder fæder sƿa he ærest sceop eorðebearnumheofon to hrofe þa middab geard moncynnes ƿeard ece drihtent efter teodefyrumonfolden frea ælmihtig halig scyppeod
