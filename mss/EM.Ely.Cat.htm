Writ

Date: s. xii/xiii

Summary:

Two passages in Old English in Book 2 of the Historia Eliensis Insulae; the same Old English passages are in CTC O. 2. 1 (1105) of s. xii2 (Ker 1957, item 93). Manuscript not localised.

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fol. 72r

      Title (A.45): Cnut's Song

      Incipit: (fol. 72r) Merie sungen ðe muneches binnen ely. ða chut. ching reu ðer by. Roweþ chites noer the lant.ond here we þes muneches sæng

      Explicit: (fol. 72r) ondhere we þes muneches sæng

      Note: Writ etc. in bk.2 of the Historia Eliensis Insulae

      Bibliography:

        Ker 1957, item 113

        Stewart 1848, p. 202

  2. Item: fol. 77r

      Title (B.15.1.94): Charters: Royal Charters: Writ of King Edward, Christ Church, Canterbury Sawyer 1088

      Incipit: (fol. 77r) Ædward cyninc gret ealle mine biscopas

      Explicit: (fol. 77r) þæs ic him geunnon habbe god eow healde

      Note: Writ etc. in bk.2 of the Historia Eliensis Insulae

      Bibliography:

        Harmer 1952, no.49

        Ker 1957, item 113

        Stewart 1848, p. 202

        Sawyer 1968, no.1100
