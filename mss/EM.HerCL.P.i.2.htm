Records of shire-moot and land purchase

Date: s. ximed

Summary:

Copied into blank space at the end of a quire, two documents: fol. 134rv, record of a shire-moot during the reign of Cnut, and fol. 135 a land purchase datable 1043-46. Ker proposes: 'The manuscript was no doubt at Hereford when the OE documents were entered' (1957, p. 156).

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fols 134r-v

      Title (B.15.5.22): Charters: Bishops' Charters: Documents: Dispute between Edwin and his mother

      Incipit: (fol. 134r) Her swutelað on ðissum gewrite þæt an scirgemot sæt æt ægelnoðes stane

      Note: The record of the shire-moot in the reign of Cnut
