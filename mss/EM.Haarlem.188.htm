Continuous Psalter-Gloss

Date: s. ximed

Summary:

A fragment of a Gallican Psalter with occasional readings from the Romanum, with continuous interlinear Old English glosses, from Pss. 119-22. Part of the central bifolium of a quire, removed from the binding of a book in the library (Ker 1976, p. 122). Fragments from the same manuscript are Cambridge, Pembroke College, 312 and Sondershausen, Schlossmuseum, Hs Br. 1. Place of production unknown.
