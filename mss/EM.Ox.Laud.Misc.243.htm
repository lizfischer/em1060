Cædmon’s Hymn

Date: s. xii2

Summary:

Main text is a copy of the Latin version of Bede’s Historia ecclesiastica. Cædmon’s Hymn is on a tab, attached to the margin later, probably in the main text hand. Place of production not known.

Manuscript Items:
