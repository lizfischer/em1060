Writ

Date: s. xii2

Summary:

Two passages in Old English in Book 2 of the Historia Eliensis Insulae; the same Old English passages are in the Ely Cathedral writ of s. xii/xiii (Ker 1957, item 113). Manuscript not localised.

Manuscript Items:
  1. Item: fols 73-74
    Title (A.45): Cnut's Song

    Addition: (fols 73v-74r) Merie sungen ðe muneches binnen ely. ða chut ching reu ðer | by. Roƿeþ chites noer the land. and here ƿve þes muneches sæng

    Text Language: English

    Bibliography:

    Ker 1957, p. 137

    Wright and Hollis 1998

  2. Item: fol. 79r
    Title (B.15.1.106): Charters: Royal Charters: Writ of King Edward, Christ Church, Canterbury Sawyer 1088: Old English writ of king Edward announcing that he has appointed Wulfric to the office of abbot of Ely

    Rubric (initial): stabilitas locia rege roborata

    Incipit: (fol. 79r) ÆDǷærdus cyning gret ælle mine biscopes ˥ mine eorlas

    Explicit: (fol. 79r) god eoƿ gehealde ('item de eodem' follows the Old English)

    Text Language: English

    Bibliography:

    Ker 1957, p. 137

    Wright and Hollis 1998
