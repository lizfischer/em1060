Continuous Psalter-gloss

Date: s. ximed

Summary:

A strip from a single leaf of a Gallican Psalter with occasional readings from the Romanum, with continuous interlinear Old English glosses. It contains parts of Psalms 69- 79 with glosses. Other fragments from the same manuscript are Cambridge, Pembroke College, 312 and Haarlem, Stadsbibliotheek, 188 F 53.
